Our purpose

Protecting a park that belongs to the whole community.

Friends of Fort Getty Park is forming an email list for neighbors, visitors, and supporters who want practical updates about the park, public meetings, volunteer opportunities, and ways to help.

This first website is intentionally focused: welcome people, gather interest, and make the group easy to contact while the organization grows.
